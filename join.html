Getting Involved with UTRA

There are various ways to get involved with UTRA.

Attend our events

UTRA hosts regular events where all students are welcome. This includes Robonars: robotics seminars and workshops, and our annual UTRAHacks.

Join a subteam

UTRA comprises five robotics subteams. Join a subteam to build the robot and head to competition!

Join the executives

Love using robots but not building them? That's alright! Join UTRA as an executive in the Marketing, Outreach, Web, or UTRAHack teams.

Follow UTRA on Instagram

Stay updated with our latest robots, achievements, competition highlights, and recruitment opportunities by following us on Instagram.

Have questions? Feel free to send us a DM—we’d love to hear from you!

Reach out to us!

We typically recruit new members at the start of the fall and winter terms, with announcements posted on our Instagram. Check there for updates on how to join!

If you miss the recruitment window, no worries—just send us a DM or email. We’re always happy to welcome new members to the team!
