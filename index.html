UTRA: Innovating the Future of Robotics

The University of Toronto Robotics Association (UTRA) brings together passionate students to design, build, and compete in robotics. From AI to mechanical design, we shape the future of robotics engineering at U of T.

    • Join Us

    Celebrating 20 years of innovation

    For two decades, UTRA has been at the forefront of robotics innovation at the University of Toronto. What started as a small group of passionate students has grown into a vibrant community of engineers, programmers, and thinkers, pushing the boundaries of technology and robotics.

    Join us as we celebrate our achievements, honor our alumni, and look ahead to the future of robotics excellence.
