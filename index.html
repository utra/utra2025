UTRA: Innovating the Future of Robotics

Established in 2004, the University of Toronto Robotics Association (UTRA) is the premier robotics student group at the University of Toronto.

Learn More

Celebrating 20 years of innovation

For two decades, UTRA has been at the forefront of robotics innovation at the University of Toronto. What started as a small group of passionate students has grown into a vibrant community of engineers, programmers, and thinkers, pushing the boundaries of technology and robotics.

Join us as we celebrate our achievements, honor our alumni, and look ahead to the future of robotics excellence.

Want to get involved?

UTRA needs you!

UTRA is the perfect place to dive into robotics, learn new skills, and work on cool projects with a team. Whether you’re into coding, building, or just curious about robotics, you’ll get hands-on experience and a chance to compete in exciting competitions. No experience? No problem—just bring your enthusiasm, and we’ll help you get started!
